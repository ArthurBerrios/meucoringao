Taça Competência

The Cidade de São Paulo Cup, better known as the São Paulo Cup, was created by the São Paulo Football Federation in 1942, and were played until 1952, to determine a champion among the clubs in the capital (more precisely, among the so-called Trio de Ferro) , Portuguesa de Desportos and Santos.

Later there was another Cup Cidade de São Paulo in a few years; randomly played within and during the São Paulo Soccer Championship.

The RSSSF affirms the existence of the 1943, 1947, 1948, 1949, 1950, 1951, and 1952 editions. SPFCpédia, the official encyclopedia of São Paulo, records the 1944 edition. For other editions (1942, 1945 and 1946) , no sources were presented
